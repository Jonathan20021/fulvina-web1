# Design

## Theme

Executive healthcare engineering. White and cool-gray content surfaces, deep graphite navigation, SCH blue for primary action and selected states, support green only for service-ready or resolved states, amber/red for operational risk.

## Color Tokens

- `--ink`: #101923
- `--muted`: #52616f
- `--line`: #d9e2ea
- `--page`: #f5f8fb
- `--surface`: #ffffff
- `--graphite`: #111827
- `--blue`: #0666b3
- `--blue-strong`: #034f8f
- `--cyan`: #20a6d8
- `--green`: #16a34a
- `--amber`: #d97706
- `--red`: #dc2626

## Typography

Use `Aptos`, `Segoe UI`, `Arial`, sans-serif for product UI and public pages. Headings use strong weights with tight but readable line-height. Product tables and numbers use tabular figures.

## Layout

Public pages use a 1200-1280px max-width container, image-led sections, clear SEO headings and varied rhythm. The CRM uses a dark compact sidebar, sticky top utility bar, dense tables, side panels and 8px radius controls.

## Components

Buttons, status chips, panels, forms, tables, quote line items and ticket timelines share one vocabulary. Cards are used for repeated records and operational summaries only. Avoid nested cards and decorative gradients.

## Media

Use downloaded SCH assets from `assets/media`: installation photos for hero/proof, equipment and medical gases images for service sections, hospital logos for projects, and authorized brand logos for trust.

## Motion

Small product motion only: 150-220ms hover, focus, disclosure and panel transitions. Respect reduced motion.
